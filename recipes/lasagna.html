Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is one of my absolute favorite dishes for a weeknight dinner. It's comforting and really fills you up so that you'll have energy to tackle the rest of your day. Normally you'd just buy a microwaveable version and eat that, but this lasagna will help you kick that habit. Here's how we're going to go about making it:

Ingredients

  • Meat of your choice (Italian sausage or lean ground beef
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Yellow onion
  • 2 Cloves of garlic
  • 1 Can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 Cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 Cans of tomato paste
  • 2 Tbsp of white sugar
  • Parmesan
  • Mozarella
  • Ricotta
  • 1 Egg
  • Fresh parsley
  • Dried basil
  • Salt
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Fennel seeds
  • Black pepper
